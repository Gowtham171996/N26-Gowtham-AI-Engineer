How to use Moneybeam?
MoneyBeam enables you to send money to your contacts instantly, without having to enter their bank details. You can also request a MoneyBeam from your contacts, in order to receive money. To use MoneyBeam, you and your contacts have to be visible as N26 users.

Sending a MoneyBeam: 
Copy link
Select Send Money,
In the contact list, select a recipient with an N26 icon on their profile
Enter the amount and select Continue
Enter an optional reference text or GIF* and select Continue
Enter your confirmation PIN or confirm it with Biometrics (E.g. FaceID or Fingerprint)
Confirm the transfer on the review screen
In case you are sending a MoneyBeam via Web app, you’ll then need to either Confirm or Cancel the MoneyBeam via push notification in the app from your paired device. Once the MoneyBeam was confirmed in the mobile app, we can't reverse it or change the recipient's details.

Instant Payment notifications
Copy link
When you send money with an instant payment solution (with an Instant SEPA transfer, MoneyBeam, or Bizum (in Spain only), you’ll receive a notification as soon as the beneficiary’s bank confirms that the funds have arrived in their account. If we don’t receive this confirmation within 10 seconds, your transfer will be automatically canceled, and the money will be restored to your N26 account. This also applies to standing orders and future-dated Instant Transfers.

How to add a new recipient?
Copy link
From the Home screen choose the Send money tab
On the contact list screen, simply select the + icon at the top right corner
Select MoneyBeam 
Enter the recipient's name and phone number or email address
Alternatively, you can scan a QR code to input the recipient’s details
Enter the amount and select Continue,
Next, proceed by entering the optional reference text or GIF
Enter your confirmation PIN or confirm it with Biometrics (E.g. FaceID or Fingerprint)
Confirm the MoneyBeam on the review screen.
Requesting a MoneyBeam:
Copy link
Select Add Money
Tap Request from friends
Select a contact from your list or add one by entering their phone number and email address
Enter an amount and a reference
As soon as your contact confirms your request, you will receive the amount you asked for
